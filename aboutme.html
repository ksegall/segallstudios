Sara Segall

I was born in Jacksonville, Florida and have lived in Central Florida the majority of my life. As a young child I treasured my art supplies. My mother would ask me to clean up after working on an art project and I would organize my crayons, markers and colored pencils according to the different hues. I remember my mother showing friends and family how my crayons would look before and after I started a project. My artwork would have to be perfect in every way. I would spend my days coloring with my grandmother or creating a craft with my mom. I, like most artists, are my own critic. With every task I aspire to greatness. This has been my motto.

After graduating from the University of South Florida I went to work at a t-shirt printing company that did graphic designing for corporations. This reshaped my thinking about my career life. I knew that I needed to follow my heart so I applied for the graphic design program at Seminole State. I have created videos to help a non-profit organization and graphic designs to promote businesses.
